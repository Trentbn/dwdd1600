FAQ

What is the best mode to shoot in?
It depends on what kind of shot you are trying to get. Manual is great for controlling every aspect of the photo. Aperture priority is great for when aperture is your main concern. Shutter speed priority is great for when shutter speed is the most important aspect. And of course auto is good if you're just trying to snap a quick photo and don't need specific settings
What does shooting in RAW mean?
Shooting in RAW specifies what type of file to save the photo as. As the name suggests, RAW saves every detail captured orignally, whithout compression. This is great for editing your photos, as all the details in the highlights and shadows are saved which allows for greater flexibility in editing.
